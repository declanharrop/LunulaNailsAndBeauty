IBX

IBX is a revolutionary strengthening treatment for fingers or toes.

 

This innovative two-part system offers revolutionary benefits and can be used under gel polish or as a natural nail growth treatment on its own.

Benefits:

- It creates a protective shield for the nail

- It reduces white spots that may occur with the continued use of gel polish

- It is used to repair severe nail damage to set the stage for growth

- It strengthens the upper layers of the nail plate allowing nail growth

- With multiple treatments, it fills up deep grooves resulting in a smoother nail plate

- It greatly improves your natural nail colour and appearance
