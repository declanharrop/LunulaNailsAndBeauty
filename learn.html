Here at Lunula Nails and Beauty, we are proud to be able to offer Cuccio Effect Education. Alicia received her Cuccio Educator certificate in 2016, which complimented her numerous other qualifications spread over all aspects of beauty therapy. As successful leaders in the nail industry for over 30 years, Cuccio are providers of fabulous nail care products. We pride ourselves on our ability to provide you with quality training and support to help you to continue to grow your business.

 

Alicia’s aim is to nurture you through your first steps into the industry and to provide ongoing training and masterclasses, tailoring the training around you. All of our courses are accredited by the guild and you will receive a comprehensive training manual, with certificates being issued upon the successful completion of written and practical tests.

Our training kits contain full sizes of product to ensure that you not only have enough product to carry out the course, but also enough to practice at home and to use thereafter. You will automatically receive a 10% discount on the price of your kit from booking with Alicia at Lunula and will have access to exclusive Lunula Student prices of Cuccio products in the future.

 

For more details on what each of our courses offer, feel free to email admin@lunula.studio and we will be happy to give you all the relevant information you’ll need to know.
